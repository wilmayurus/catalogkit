{# ── FAQ ── #}

Common questions

What counts as a "build"?
Every time you click Confirm & Build Catalog, that uses one build. Viewing the flipbook or downloading the PDF is always free and doesn't count.
Can I edit a catalog after building it?
Once built, a catalog is locked so your customers always see the correct version. To make changes, create a new catalog — this uses one build from your plan.
How do I upgrade?
Message us on WhatsApp and let us know how you'd like to pay — card (Visa/Mastercard), mobile banking, internet banking, or cash on delivery.

If you pay by bank or mobile transfer, WhatsApp us a screenshot of the receipt.
If you choose cash on delivery, we'll confirm with you first — once that's sorted, you WhatsApp us and we'll activate your plan. We'll confirm within a few hours.
When do my builds reset?
On the 1st of every calendar month, your build count resets to zero automatically.
