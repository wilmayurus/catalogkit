How it works

Ready in 3 simple steps

No computer needed. Works on any Android or iPhone, even on slow mobile data.

  1. 1
    📸

    Take photos of your products

    Use your phone camera to take photos of your products — bilums, produce, clothing, crafts, anything.

  2. 2
    ⬆️

    Upload & set prices

    Upload your photos and type the price under each one. Your catalog builds automatically — no design skills needed.

  3. 3
    📲

    Share on WhatsApp

    Tap the WhatsApp button and send your catalog link to customers. They can browse and place orders instantly.

Accessibility & inclusion

Designed for every vendor

CatalogKit is built so anyone can use it — regardless of reading ability, technical experience, or the phone they have.

  • 📖

    Plain, simple English

    No technical words. No confusing menus. Just clear, everyday language written for real people.

    Tap the "Aa" button on any page to make all text bigger and easier to read — great for low vision.

    No app download needed. Opens in your phone browser — even older or lower-cost Android models.

    Built to WCAG AA accessibility standards — works with screen readers and keyboard navigation.
