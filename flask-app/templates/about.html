About CatalogKit

A free digital tool built in Port Moresby to help PNG market vendors and small businesses reach more customers through WhatsApp.

What is CatalogKit?

CatalogKit lets you take photos of your products with your phone and turn them into a beautiful digital catalogue — a flipbook your customers can browse online, plus a PDF they can save or print.

Once your catalogue is ready, you share one link on WhatsApp. Your customers can see all your products, prices, and how to order — without needing to download any app.

Why we built it

Papua New Guinea has thousands of talented market vendors and small business owners selling great products — but most don't have a way to show their customers what they sell online.

Printed catalogues are expensive. Building a website takes tech skills most vendors don't have. And customers already use WhatsApp every day.

CatalogKit solves this simply: take photos, upload them, share a link. No computer. No design skills. No printing bills.

Who we are

CatalogKit was built in Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea, by a local team that understands the daily reality of PNG market vendors and small business owners.

We are proud to be a PNG-built product, designed specifically for the needs of PNG vendors — not adapted from something built overseas.
