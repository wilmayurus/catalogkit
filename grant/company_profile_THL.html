About Us

Trey Holdings Limited was established in 2018 and formally incorporated in 2020 by esteemed IT professionals in Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea. Our primary objective is to deliver superior Information and Communications Technology (ICT) products, custom software applications, and tech-driven business enablement services to corporate entities, public organizations, and community enterprises.

Our core technical expertise spans the entire spectrum of modern networks and digital architecture — encompassing the design, supply, installation, and configuration of wireless networks, LAN switches, WAN/VPN routing infrastructure, advanced IT security firewalls, structured cabling, and robust power protection systems (UPS).

In line with our commitment to digital transformation and local empowerment, Trey Holdings Limited has successfully expanded into custom platform development. This includes CatalogKit (www.catalogkit.org), our specialised mobile-first digital product catalog infrastructure built specifically to bridge the digital divide for PNG market vendors, informal traders, and micro-MSMEs. Alongside our primary ICT operations, we provide a reliable suite of auxiliary corporate services including architectural design and tender submissions, GPS vehicle tracking installations, office stationery supply, and comprehensive audio-visual event management solutions.
