The Problem We Solve

PNG market vendors and informal traders have no affordable way to showcase their products digitally. Most cannot afford a website, and existing tools require technical skills or expensive data plans. Products go unseen beyond the physical market stall — limiting income and growth.

Our Solution

CatalogKit removes every barrier. It is permanently free, requires no technical skills, works on slow 3G data, and delivers a professional-looking digital catalog and PDF within minutes of uploading photos. Sharing is done via WhatsApp — the tool every vendor already uses.

Done-For-You Service

For vendors who are not comfortable with technology, Trey Holdings trains field agents who visit market stalls in person and build the vendor's first catalog alongside them — for a flat K50 cash fee on the day.

  • No smartphone knowledge required
  • Agent sits with the vendor at their stall
  • 50+ agent visits planned in Year 1
  • Prioritises women and elderly vendors
